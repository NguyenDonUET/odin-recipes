Lasagna Food

Description

This classic lasagna recipe features layers of tender pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and gooey mozzarella cheese. Each bite delivers a comforting blend of savory flavors and satisfying textures.

Perfect for family dinners or special occasions, this lasagna is both hearty and delicious. Serve it with a side salad and garlic bread for a complete Italian-inspired meal.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • Fresh basil or parsley, for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a skillet, brown the ground beef over medium heat. Drain excess fat.
  4. Add marinara sauce to the beef and simmer for 5 minutes.
  5. Spread a layer of meat sauce in the bottom of a baking dish.
  6. Layer noodles, meat sauce, and cheese. Repeat layers, finishing with cheese on top.
  7. Bake for 25-30 minutes, until cheese is bubbly and golden.
  8. Let cool for a few minutes, garnish with basil or parsley, and serve.
