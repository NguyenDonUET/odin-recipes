Judy's Strawberry Pretzel Salad

Description

This three-layer strawberry pretzel dessert salad includes a pretzel crust, cream cheese center, and strawberry top.

Perfect for family dinners or special occasions, this lasagna is both hearty and delicious. Serve it with a side salad and garlic bread for a complete Italian-inspired meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups crushed pretzels
  • ¾ cup butter, melted
  • 4 ½ tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix crushed pretzels, melted butter, and 4 ½ tablespoons sugar in a bowl. Press into the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish to form a crust.
  3. Bake the crust for 10 minutes, then set aside to cool completely.
  4. In a separate bowl, beat softened cream cheese and 1 cup sugar until smooth. Fold in whipped topping.
  5. Spread the cream cheese mixture evenly over the cooled pretzel crust. Refrigerate until set, about 30 minutes.
  6. In another bowl, dissolve strawberry gelatin in boiling water. Stir in frozen strawberries until mixture thickens slightly.
  7. Pour the strawberry mixture over the cream cheese layer. Refrigerate until fully set, at least 2 hours.
  8. Slice and serve chilled.
