World's Best Potato Salad

Description

This best-ever potato salad is the creamiest, richest potato salad you will ever try. The secret is in the homemade dressing. I get nothing but raves every time I make this.

Perfect for family dinners or special occasions, this lasagna is both hearty and delicious. Serve it with a side salad and garlic bread for a complete Italian-inspired meal.

Ingredients

  • 6 medium potatoes
  • Â½ cup white sugar
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 1 teaspoon cornstarch
  • salt to taste

Steps

  1. Peel and dice the potatoes into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Boil the potatoes in salted water until tender, about 10-15 minutes. Drain and let cool.
  3. In a saucepan, combine sugar, eggs, cornstarch, and a pinch of salt. Cook over low heat, stirring constantly, until thickened. Remove from heat and let cool.
  4. In a large bowl, combine the cooled potatoes and dressing. Mix gently to coat.
  5. Chill in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour before serving.
